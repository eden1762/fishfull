KNOW YOUR FISH

看到這些特徵，就比較好認Quick ways to recognize it

1

魚身偏紅、背部較深Red body with a deeper-toned back

新鮮魚身有光澤，不是暗沉乾澀。Fresh skin should look glossy rather than dull and dry.

2

眼睛清亮、魚鰓鮮紅Clear eyes and bright gills

買之前也可以直接請魚販一起確認。Ask the fishmonger to check these signs with you.

3

肉細清甜，適合清蒸Delicate, clean-tasting meat

想吃原味就清蒸，想要香氣就乾煎。Steam for a clean flavor or pan-fry for a crisp finish.

COOK IT

今晚怎麼煮How to cook it tonight

先從最穩的薑絲清蒸開始，魚販也能用三句話快速分享。Start with a simple ginger-steamed version that is easy to explain at the counter and easy to cook at home.

薑絲清蒸赤鯮｜約 15 分鐘Ginger-steamed sea bream · about 15 min

  1. 魚身擦乾，兩面各劃兩刀。Pat the fish dry and score both sides.
  2. 鋪薑片，水滾後放入蒸 8～10 分鐘。Add ginger and steam for 8–10 minutes after the water boils.
  3. 魚肉可輕鬆撥開就起鍋，放蔥絲、淋少量醬油。Remove when the meat flakes easily; finish with scallion and a little soy sauce.
